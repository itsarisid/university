# Timetable Management System
Managing timetables can be a complex and time-consuming task for educational institutions. At OpenEduCat, our Timetable Management System simplifies and automates the scheduling process, ensuring that your school's operations run smoothly and efficiently.

## Explore the Features of Our Timetable Management System
Discover how our Timetable Management System simplifies scheduling with intuitive tools, automated processes, and seamless integration. From easy timetable creation and real-time updates to customizable templates and instant notifications, our system is designed to streamline your institution’s operations and enhance efficiency.

- #### Easy Timetable Management
    Schedule and manage timetables effortlessly with intuitive tools and features.

- #### Pre-set Timetables
    Admins can quickly set timetables for all classes for any term, streamlining scheduling.

- #### Custom Class Timings
    Create various timing sets for different periods to match your institution’s needs.

- #### Simple Subject Allocation
    Allocate subjects to teachers with ease, ensuring smooth class management.

- #### Teacher Access
    Teachers can view their timetables from their login to plan their schedules effectively.

- #### Student Timetable Access
    Students can check their weekly or daily schedules to stay organized and informed.

- #### Classroom Management
    Effortlessly manage classroom allocations to optimize space and resources.

- #### Institutional Reports
    Generate detailed timetable reports for analysis or display on notice boards.

- #### Instant Swap Notifications
    Automatically notify teachers instantly when timetable swaps are made.
